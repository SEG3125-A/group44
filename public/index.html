FAQ

How do I rent a car?

To rent a car with us, you can use the 'Rent' page on our website, book in person at one of our locations, or call/email our customer service team via the button at the bottom left of our website.

What is the minimum age to rent a car?

The minimum age to rent a car with us is 18. However, there is an extra insurance fee for drivers under the age of 25.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept Visa and MasterCard credit cards only.

Can I add an additional driver to my rental?

Yes, additional drivers can be added on vehicle pickup for a small fee. Additional drivers must bring their id and fill out a registration form on vehicle pickup.

Can I return my rental car if the (Y)OUR CAR location is closed?

Yes, we offer after-hours drop-off. Just park the car at any drop-off parking spot and drop the car key into the key drop-off box. Ask a representative for additional info when you pick up your rental car.
